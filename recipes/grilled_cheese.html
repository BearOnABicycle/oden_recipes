Grilled Cheese

Don't miss this all American favorite. No childhood is complete without a healthy dose of grilled cheese. You can even make this dish more nutrient rich by using whole grain bread and for the veggie folks you can try a vegan alternative cheese. I would reccoment violife chedder!

Ingredients

  • American Cheese (one slice per sandwich)
  • Bread (two slices per sandwich)
  • Butter or vegan alternative (about 2 Tbsp)

Steps

  1. Butter two slices of bread on one side
  2. Head a sauce pan on medium high and add hald Teaspoon on butter
  3. once pan is heated add one slice of bead butter side down
  4. Add cheese to slice in pan
  5. Place other slice on top of cheese with the butter side out
  6. After aproximitely 3 minutes flip the sandwitch and grill other side another 3 minutes
  7. If needed keep flipping until both sides are grilled to your preference
