Scrambled "Eggs"

This is a healthy and filling dish that can be served any time of the day, but fits especially well at breakfast. With 8oz of tofu you will get your fill of protein. You can even mix it up by adding more of your favorite veggies. I like to add herlume tomatos and when I'm feeling a little crazy some vegan feta.

Ingredients

  • Tofu (8oz)
  • Olive Oil (1 Tbsp)
  • Red Onion (1/4 copped)
  • Bell Pepper (1/2 thinly sliced)
  • Kale (2 cups loosely chopped)
  • Tumeric (1/4 tsp)
  • Garlic Powder (1/2 tsp)

Instructions

  1. Press dry your tofu. This can be accomplished with paper towel and two plates if you do not have a tofu press.
  2. Heat a sauce pan to medium heat and add olive oil.
  3. While pan is heating combine tofu, tumeric and garlic powder to mixing bowel and mix together with heands. Try to get the tofu as crumbly as possible while thoroughly mixing seasonings.
  4. Add chopped red onion and bell peppers to pan and to stir. Cook until starting to brown.
  5. Add crumbled tofu mix to pan and stir occasionally. Tofu containes a lot of water so be sure to cook off as much as needed for desired texture.
  6. Once tofu is near desired texture add kale. Mixe and cook untill rich dark green.
  7. Salt and pepper to tast and service with bagel or toast to make a tofu scrable sandwich
