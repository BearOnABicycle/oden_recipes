Veggie Fajita

This deliciouse and veggie filled favorite can be completely cooked on the grill. There is no better way to serve a healthy dinner to a hungry family then will this classic dish. Who dosn't love grilled peppers, onions and mushrooms? Just dont forget the toppings and add your own to make this dish even better. My personal favorite is to add chopped red cabbage. I love the crunch and apparently red cabbage is one of the most micronutrient dense foods in the world!

Ingredients

    For Fajitas:

  • multi-colored peppers(3 stemmed and sliced into strips)
  • portobello mushrooms(3 stemmed and wiped clean)
  • red onion(1 sliced into wedges)
  • avocado oil(2 Tbsp)
  • garlic cloves(2 chopped)
  • chili powder(1/2 tsp)
  • cumin(1/2 tsp)
  • sea salt(1/2 tsp)
  • balsamic vinegar
  • limes(2 sliced into wedges)
  • black pepper(to taste)

    • For serving:

  • tortillas(8 flour or corn)
  • guacamole
  • pico de gallo
  • cilantro
  • tomatillo salsa

Steps

  1. Heat grill to medium heat and place a skillet or grill pan inside.
  2. Whisk the avocado oil, garlic, chili powder, cumin, salt and black pepper in a small bowl.
  3. Arrange the peppers on a tray, then the mushrooms and onions on a rimmed plate.
  4. Drizzel 1 Tbsp of the avocado oil mix onto the peppers. Drizzel the remaining avocado oil mix over the mushrooms and onions. Add a splash of balsamic vinegar to the mushrooms and use your hands to coat all sides.
  5. Grill the peppers in the skillet for 8 to 10 minutes. The goale is to get a char on all edges. If needed seperate and grill in seperate batches.
  6. Grill the mushrooms and onions dirrectly on the grill for about 4 minutes per side.
  7. Remove everything from the grill. Squeeze the lime over the peppers and add salt, pepper and chili powder to taste.
  8. Slice the mushrooms into strips and then place all the veggies on a serving dish.
  9. Serve as a build your own with tortilla, gucamole, cilantro, limes and salsa.
